## VECTOR_DIMS {#GUID-73D916C1-5ABE-4ED2-9BB6-018AF4540C62}

`VECTOR_DIMS` returns the number of dimensions of a vector as a `NUMBER`. `VECTOR_DIMS` is synonymous with `VECTOR_DIMENSION_COUNT`.
